Brownie

Description

Brownies are a simple yet delicious treat that are a staple in most bakeries. However, it's simple to make even at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs + water
  • powdered sugar
  • unsweetened cocoa powder
  • oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanila extract

Steps

  1. Mix together the dry and wet ingredients in two separate bowls
  2. Combine the wet and dry ingredients
  3. Pour the batter into an 8Ã—8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper
  4. Transfer the pan to a 325-degree oven and bake for 40 to 45 minutes
